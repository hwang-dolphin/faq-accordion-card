FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

No more than four members. Each member must sign in.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

Enter your email and press 'forgot password'. Enter the six digit code for verification.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You have a period of 14 days to cancel your subscription.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes, we do provide phone and chat support for our customers.
